Skiing is a winter Sport which uses skis to glide on snow. In Xscapade, there are 4 main long indoor skiing slopes, and wide outdoor skiing playground during winter. There is also a dedicated lesson slope for beginners and to build their skills and confidence before progressing to the main slope. Many great-value-offer for skiing lesson:


  • £15 one-hour lesson (include one-hour train lesson, clothing and equipment hire)
  • £35 three hours lesson (include three-hours train lesson, clothing and equipment hire and 30 mins free for slope passes in the future)
  • £60 four hours lesson (include four-hours train lesson, clothing and equipment hire and 1.5 hours free for slope passes in the future)
  • Hourly slope passes cost £24.99 adults; £20.99 juniors.

Each equipment hire per hour is £3.
